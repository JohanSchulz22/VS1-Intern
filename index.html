01

About VS1

VS1 builds institutional financial infrastructure on the XRP Ledger (XRPL) — focused on tokenization, lending, and regulated market structure. We are bringing fixed-income capital markets on-chain, starting in Georgia and expanding across the region.

We are not a crypto startup. We are a regulated financial infrastructure company that happens to run on blockchain rails.
02

Role Overview

We are looking for a sharp, curious intern to support across business development, product research, and go-to-market execution. This role sits where capital markets, blockchain technology, and regulated financial infrastructure meet.

You will work directly with the founding team and be exposed to real deal flow, investor onboarding, and product strategy from day one.

03
